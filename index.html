Elita Restaurant

Savor the rich flavors of Uzbekistan at our in-house restaurant. From hearty national breakfasts of samsa and non to traditional plov, shashlik, and lagman — our kitchen celebrates the finest of Uzbek cuisine. European dishes are also available for international guests. Breakfast, lunch, and dinner served daily. Banquet arrangements available for groups and events.
